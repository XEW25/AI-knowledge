## Vault layers
The knowledge is refined through four layers (see [[90 System/AGENTS|the operating guide]] for the rules):
- **Inbox** — capture / intake (currently empty).
- **Raw** — source-of-truth artifacts (PDFs, clippings), kept close to original form.
- **Sources** — processed one-per-source notes.
- **Wiki** — the persistent knowledge layer: `Concepts / Entities / Topics / Syntheses`.
- **Maps** — navigation hubs (this page and the two cluster MOCs).
